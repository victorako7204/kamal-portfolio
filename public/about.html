I’m Muhammad Kamal — a motion designer and 3D artist who believes that great design isn’t just seen, it’s felt. For over two years, I’ve been turning raw ideas into cinematic visual experiences that make brands impossible to scroll past.

My specialty sits at the intersection of storytelling and technical precision. Whether it’s a 15-second social ad that stops thumbs mid-scroll, a product reveal that makes your audience lean in, or a full brand identity system that scales across every touchpoint — I obsess over every frame until it’s undeniable.

I work primarily in After Effects and Blender, but the tools are just the vehicle. What matters is the outcome: visuals that don’t just look good, but perform. Higher engagement. Stronger recall. More conversions. That’s the real measure of design.
