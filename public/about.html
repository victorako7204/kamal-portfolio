I am Muhammad Kamal, a creative motion designer with over 2 years of experience creating engaging visual content for brands and businesses. I specialize in motion graphics, animation, and visual storytelling using industry-standard tools like Adobe After Effects and Blender. I enjoy transforming concepts into dynamic motion experiences, whether for brand promotions, social media content, product showcases, or creative visual campaigns. Over the years, I have continued to improve my skills in both 2D and 3D animation, developing a style that is clean, modern, and visually impactful. I am passionate about creativity, detail, and delivering high-quality motion design solutions that help brands stand out.
